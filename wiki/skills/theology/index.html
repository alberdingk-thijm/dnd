Theology

Theology is the study of religion and religious practice. It encapsulates religious techniques, histories and distinctions in orthodox versus heretical practice.

Theology is a modal skill: theologians must choose a world religion to study when investing knowledge points. Theology knowledge points provide halved bonuses for religions outside of a theologian's specialty.

Clerics and paladins can study theology.

Tree

  • Religious history: provides characters with knowledge of the history of their religion
  • Prayer: allows characters to pray to the divine for aid or assistance
  • Proselytize: allows characters to encourage the adoption of their religion by others
  • Preaching: allows characters to conduct a sermon or lecture on religious teachings
  • Zealotry: improves characters' combat abilities versus enemies of their religion
